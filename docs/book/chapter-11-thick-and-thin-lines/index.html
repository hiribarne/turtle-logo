Chapter 11: Thick and Thin Lines

You can make your lines thicker or thinner.

SETWIDTH 5

SETWIDTH sets how thick the pen draws. The number is the width. 1 is thin, 5 is thick, 10 is very thick!

Try this:

CS
SETWIDTH 1
FD 60
RT 90
SETWIDTH 5
FD 60
RT 90
SETWIDTH 10
FD 60

Three lines — thin, medium, and thick!
