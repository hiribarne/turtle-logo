Chapter 2: Turn the Turtle

The turtle can turn, too.

RT 90

RT means turn right. 90 is a number of degrees.

Do you know what 90 degrees is? It is a right angle — like the corner of a book, or a door, or your desk.

Now try:

LT 90

LT means turn left.

Try some other turns:

RT 45

That is half of a right angle.

RT 180

That turns the turtle all the way around to face the opposite direction!
