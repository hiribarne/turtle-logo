Chapter 13: A Star

A star has 5 points.

For a five-pointed star, we turn 144 degrees each time. (That is a special number. It works because 144 × 5 = 720, which is two full spins!)

CS
SETPC 3
REPEAT 5 [FD 100 RT 144]

A beautiful yellow star!
