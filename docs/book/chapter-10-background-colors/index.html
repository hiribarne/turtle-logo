Chapter 10: Background Colors

You can change the background color too!

SETBG 1

SETBG means set background.

Try these:

  • SETBG 0 — black (the default)
  • SETBG 9 — blue
  • SETBG 15 — white
  • SETBG 14 — yellow

Draw a white snowflake on a blue background:

CS
SETBG 9
SETPC 0
REPEAT 6 [FD 80 BK 80 RT 60]
