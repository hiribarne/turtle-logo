Chapter 1: Your First Steps

Type this and press ENTER:

FD 50

The turtle went forward 50 steps!

FD means forward. The number tells the turtle how far to go.

Try these one at a time:

FD 100
FD 10
FD 200

What happens when the number is bigger? The turtle goes farther!

Now try going backward:

BK 50

BK means back.

The turtle walks backward, but it still faces the same way.
